About Me

I have recently started year two of web development at the University of Staffordshire. I am passionate about all aspects of web development and design and plan to create a succesful career in the near future following completion of my degree. I am primarily interested in front end development but am currently learning and improving my knowldge in back end in order to become a full stack developer in the future.

I am determined and dedicated to pursuing my career in the web dev world. I enjoy keeping active when I am not stuck behind a computer screen, but computers have always interested me and this career provides the perfect mix between programming and creativity. What can I offer you? I can build websites, I am extremely passionate about it and this gives me the motivation every day to explore new depths and learn new techniques.
